The regions of Greater Kolhm

Divided into nine regions amongst the Empire, Greater Kolhm still has a substantial wilderness, The Hinderfold. The Hinderfold is home to wild folk, thick forests, and beasts still yet to be descovered.

Holm

Captial: Korvin

Rahms major trading port, Korvin was founded in the bay of Holm. Tucked away in the northern most point of the Lastian Sea, Korvin bridges the sea and land trade routes. Housing multiple drydocks, Korvin also has the largest known ship fleet in all of Greater Kolhm, both military and commercial, Korvin has alwasy been a key point of interest in all of Greater Kolhm's wars and historical events.

Mena

Capital: Levenin

A quiet city situated in a valley carved by a glacier thousands of years prior. Having trade routes to the north and south, connecting the northern military regions to the southern agricultural cities, linking important resources among the eastern lands. Levenin is also home to the Rahmian master masons, having abundance of quarry sites in the surrounding mountains.

Nokke

Capital: New Haven

New Haven is home to the libraries of man. Holding the history of Greater Kolhm and the only known surviving scrolls of the First Family, Gods that surposely carved and shaped the world. Priding itself in knowledge and intelligence, New Haven has hosted many of Greater Kolhm's wisest leaders and politicians. New Haven's vast libraries are found high in the city, guarded by sentinels whoare chosen and trained from birth.

Rahm

Capital: Bastion

The capital city of Bastion has stood as the epi-center of man for thousands of years. Fueled by steam and mechanical abominations, Bastion was founded by the creationists, humans that have lost their souls to the power of steam and moving parts. Raising massive temples known as mechanius-harbors, birthplace of the Soil Reapers, mobile funaces with a sole purpose of fueling mans greed.

Revh

Capital: Junde

Nested in the eastern region of Greater Kolhm, Junde stands as the empires military capital. Surrounded by the bleak Myress Mire, Junde's fortifications have stood the test of time. Home to the legendary Rahmian Legion, born on the practice fields of Junde's marshland outskirts, young initiatives are put to the test in one of Greater Kolhms harshest enviroments, creating only the toughest soldiers.

Rinen

Capital: Cadence

Rahms capital of religious scholars, studying the acts of the First Family. Cavernous cathedrals tower over the coastal city, renowned for the elegant pipe organs and art decorating the interior of the walls and ceilings, depicting the god Aenimus, the scholar of the mind and vocalist of the heavens. Cadence is known as a common pilgramage site for all those that pledge their lives to the mind god.

Tor

Capital: Edge of Tor

Home to the emporer of Rahm, the broken city boasts the foundations of the grand palace of Tor. Built of the cliffs of the Aequaasian north sea, Edge of Tor once held a fortified peninsula with walls and battlements built to the cliff face, but after years of erosion the northern parts of the city have fallen victim to the icey waters below. Reinforced, the city now stands bordered by southern plains and a northern sea.
